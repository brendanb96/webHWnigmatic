Banana Pancake Recipe
(Adapted from ADDEAN1 on allrecipes.com)

Ingredients:
(9 Items)
1 cup all-purpose flour
1 tablespoon white sugar
2 teaspoons baking powder
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 egg, beaten
1 cup milk
2 tablespoons vegetable oial
2 ripe bananas, mashed
1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract

Preparation:
(5 Minutes)
First Bowl combine flour, baking powder, and salt
Second Bowl combine egg, milk, vegetable oil, and bananas
Stir flour mixture into banana mixture until lumps decrease

Cook:
(10 Minutes)
Heat oiled pan over medium high heat
Pour or scoop batter, 1/4 cup recommended size
Cook until both sides are golden brown

Optional:
Whip cream and sliced bananas on top of stacked pancakes
