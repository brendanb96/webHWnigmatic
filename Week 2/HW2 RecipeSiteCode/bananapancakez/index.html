Banana Pancake

Ingredients

    (9 Items)

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oial
  • 2 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract

Prep and Cook

Preparation

    (5 Minutes)

  1. Combine flour, baking powder, and salt
  2. Combine egg, milk, vegetable oil, and bananas in a second bowl
  3. Stir flour mixture into banana mixture until lumps decrease

Cook

    (10 Minutes)

  1. Heat oiled pan over medium high heat
  2. Pour or scoop batter, 1/4 cup recommended size
  3. Cook until both sides are golden brown

Optional:
Whip cream and sliced bananas on top of stacked pancakes
